| p(x, y) | y = −1 | y = 1 | y = 3 |
| x = −1  |    0.1 |   0.1 | z     |
| x = 1   |    0.3 |  0.05 | 0.05  |

\begin{align}
\int p(x,y) &= 1 \\
1 &= 0.1 + 0.1 + 0.3 + 0.05 + 0.05 + z = 0.6 + z \\
z &= 1 - 0.6 = 0.4
\end{align}
